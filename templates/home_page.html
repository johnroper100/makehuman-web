Open Source Tool for Making 3D Characters

This is the official website of the MakeHuman character generation software.

MakeHuman is the free and open source software to create realistic 3d humans for illustrations, animations, games, and sculpting.

Content created with MakeHuman is licensed under the CC0 license giving artists unprecedented freedom to use their MakeHuman creations in any way they can imagine.
