Open Source Tool for Making 3D Characters

This is the official website of the MakeHuman character generation software.

MakeHuman is free and open source software used to create realistic 3D humans for illustrations, animations, games, and sculpting.

Content created with MakeHuman is licensed under the CC0 license whoch gives artists unprecedented freedom to use their MakeHuman creations in any way they can imagine.

1170 Morphings for Effective Parametric Modeling

Easy and intuitive parameters, including age, gender, height, weight, body proportions, face shapes, eyes, nose, mouth, chin, ears, neck, hands details, feet and more.

12 Years of Research In Body Topology

Since the first release, MakeHuman has used a unique mesh that has evolved through successive releases that incorporates community feedback and the results of many studies and experimentation.

Python Powered

MakeHuman is completely written in Python, the widely used scripting language used at ILM (Industrial Light and Magic) since 1996. This makes MakeHuman simple, highly portable, and completely customizablle thanks to full plugin support.
